Optimization Tips
For JPGs:

bash
convert input.png -quality 85 -resize 3000x2000 -sampling-factor 4:2:0 output.jpg
Reduces file size while maintaining 3:2 ratio

For GIFs:

bash
ffmpeg -i input.mp4 -vf "fps=15,scale=1800:1200" -loop 0 output.gif
Limits to 15 FPS for smaller files

For PNGs:

bash
pngquant --quality=80-95 --speed=1 --output output.png input.png
